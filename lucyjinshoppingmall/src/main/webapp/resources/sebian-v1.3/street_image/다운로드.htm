LWS reveals its high net worth Social Calendar for 2018. From sports, to cinema, fashion and music, those events are the ones you wouldn’t want to miss. Here is our non exhaustive though quite meaningful shortlist:

 

#1 ︱ SUPER BOWL 52

WHEN︱ February the 4th, 2018

WHERE︱ U.S. Bank Stadium, Minneapolis, USA

One cannot speak about a social calendar, without including this massive worldwide sports event. According to Sports Illustrated, this year “home-field advantage will play a role in Super Bowl 52. The Vikings are the heavy favorite in the NFC, picked to win the conference by 10 of our 11 prognosticators”. In terms of music entertainment, Justin Timberlake will be the one taking over from Lady Gaga, performing the annually awaited show in this 70.000 people massive stadium.

#2 ︱ COACHELLA

WHEN︱ April the 13th – 15th &  the 20th – 22nd, 2018

WHERE︱ California, USA

It is never too early to start preparing for the most awaited music festival of the world. This year’s lineup will include top rated artists such as Beyoncé, The Weekend, Eminem and Jamiroquai, as well as electronic music figures such as Motor City Drum Ensemble and Chris Liebing.

#3 ︱ MONACO GRAND PRIX

WHEN︱ May the 24th – 27th, 2018

WHERE︱ Monaco, France

If you happen to be in Europe this spring, don’t miss this historical event’s 76th edition, considered amongst the three most ancient and prestigious ones of the world. The circuit will extend from Albatros to Beaurivage and will hosts the world’s leading pilots such as Sebastian Vettel (Ferrari) and Sergio Perez (Force India Mercedes), who were last year’s favorites.

#4 ︱ CANNES FILM FESTIVAL

WHEN︱ May the 8th – 19th, 2018

WHERE︱ Cannes, France

This year’s prestigious red carpet will be hosting gorgeous Cate Blanchet as jury president. Following 2017’s anniversary, the  71st edition of this major event, will begin a  new period in its history. As suggested by Pierre Lescure: “We intend to renew the principles of our organisation as much as possible, while continuing to question the cinema of our age and to be present through its upheavals.”

▸ Request ticket information with top fares and benefits

#5 ︱ SUNDANCE FILM FESTIVAL

WHEN︱ January the 18th – 28th, 2018

WHERE︱ Park City, Utah, USA

As the major American independent film festival, the event has announced several intriguing additions to its program, including eight feature films, a VR experience, the NEXT Innovator’s Award juror and hosted retrospectives joining the lineup today. Screenings will take place in Park City, Salt Lake City and at Sundance Mountain Resort January 18-28.

▸ Request ticket information with top fares and benefits

 

#6 ︱ ART BASEL MIAMI

WHEN︱ December the 6th – 9th, 2018

WHERE︱ Miami, USA

The Miami Beach art show ranks as the premier art show of the Americas offering a premier platform for renowned artists and galleries, and is set to amaze its 2018’s visitors. As we just attended its 2017’s 16th edition, it is no wonder we can’t wait to see its next edition unravel our century’s major contemporary and modern artists in the most mind blowing manners.

WHERE︱ New York, USA – London, UK – Milan, Italy – Paris, France

This month’s shows will be mainly Milan and Paris based, mainly showcasing Men’s and Haute Couture collections. Don’t miss February’s awaited Women’s Fashion shows in all key cities, book your VIP seats right away with LWS!

#8 ︱ U.S. OPEN TENNIS CHAMPIONSHIP

WHEN︱ August 17th – September 9th, 2018

WHERE︱ USTA National Tennis Center, Flushing Meadows-Corona Park, New York

Another major sports worldwide event, this time for inexorable tennis lovers. Will Rafa persist as the incontestable ruler? Will Sloane continue to surprise us? Attend and revel in this incredibly elegant and fierce annual battlefield.

#9 ︱ KENTUCKY DERBY

WHEN︱ May 5th, 2018

WHERE︱ Churchill Downs, Louisville, Kentucky, USA

Visit this year’s must-visit horse racing event and let this century old event delight you with top competing horses and riders. Beware not to miss its legendary Garland of Roses and Twin Spires traditions!

#10 ︱ MASTERS GOLF TOURNAMENT

WHEN︱ April 5th – 8th, 2018

WHERE︱ Augusta National Golf Club, Augusta, Géorgie, USA

The Masters’ 82nd edition will be challenging Sergio Garcia, the defending champion who defeated Justin Rose in a playoff to win last year’s tournament, after both players finished at 9-under 279. May the best player win!
